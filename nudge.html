Nudge

iOS app made by Nikita Belosludtcev

Award winning app featured in VentureBeat, TechCrunch and The Wall Street Journal. You live and work in amazing ​places where lots of incredible things are happening all the time. So many things, in fact, that you easily miss awesome events and great ​days and ​nights out. ​Nudge is the cure to ​your​ ​"​FOMO​"​ ​(fear of missing out), transforming you to always 'in the know.' It's all about the exciting stuff happening around you (the gigs, restaurants, clubs, film openings, bars, hikes, runs, sports, exhibits, classes, and more), which is personalized to you based on what you like (interests), location and timing. If you have ever had wanderlust in an urban area, a sense of adventure or are traveling across the globe, let nudge be your guide.
